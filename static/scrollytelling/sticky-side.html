Here's why Meghan Markle is a living icon

By me

Meghan Markle is an iconic queen. She studied at Northwestern University, interned at an embassy in Argentina, and ultimately landed a role on "Suits."

She's a gifted writer and she has great style.

She makes friends easily. One of those friends supposedly introduced her to a prince.

She brought a new energy to the British royal family. She also had enough life experience to stand up for herself within the royal machine.


She pursued some really interesting philanthropic projects, such as a clothing line and a cookbook.

She was popular with the crowds when she went with her husband on international tours.

But the royal family was not nice to her. They squandered her social and philanthropic gifts. So she moved home to California.

Whatever. The U.K.'s loss is California's gain. Haters be gone!

And now she and her husband are raking in millions after signing deals with Netflix and Spotify. A true business mind and an icon.
