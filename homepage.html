Hi! I'm on the Speed and Trending team at the Journal.

I cover breaking news and trending stories. I learn something new from every story and it's so much fun.

I'm also a data nerd. I have a master's degree in data journalism from Columbia Journalism School. In addition to reporting and writing narrative stories, I learned how to analyze data, design graphics and scrape websites. (I coded this website.)

Before I joined the Journal in May 2022, I was a reporting fellow at The New York Times. I worked on the Express desk where I covered breaking news and trending stories. I also had stints at The Boston Globe and The News & Observer. I learned so much from the kind folks in those newsrooms.

I may have a New York license now but I still consider myself a Californian. If you need to find me, try going to a Manhattan spin studio or emailing me at alyssa.lukpat@wsj.com.
